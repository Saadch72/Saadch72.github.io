Who We Are

Aims Health Solutions is a privately held veterinary pharmaceutical company located in Lahore Pakistan. We are mainly importing and distributing veterinary medicines, especially for poultry. Aims Health Solutions was established in 2012 by Experienced Professionals who has served more than two decades in Pakistan Veterinary Medicine and veterinary Products Importing and Marketing Industry. We have authorized importer of “XVET GmbH Germany” and "Albors Srl Italy " for importing quality products in Pakistan. Aims Health Solutions is primarily a vitamin and mineral premix importer and distributor of various feed additives, veterinary pharmaceuticals, and other veterinary products. We cover feed additives, vaccines for poultry and large animals, veterinary pharmaceuticals, disinfectants, amino acids, vitamin + mineral premixes, and pet health care products.
